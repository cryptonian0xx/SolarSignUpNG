Why We Exist

Nigeria had the funding.
It lacked the demand data.

SolarSignupNG was created during Nigeria's Solar Power Naija initiative. Financing existed for solar companies, but no verified registry of genuine household interest existed to guide deployment. We built that registry.

💡

Supply Without Demand Intel

Previous initiatives financed solar companies without mapping where real customers existed — creating a disconnect between available capacity and actual uptake.

📋

No Verified Beneficiary List

Without a central registry, solar companies faced high acquisition costs and inefficient deployment. Our database changes that with pre-qualified, verified leads.

🔌

Invisible Demand

Most potential beneficiaries had no mechanism to express interest. They remained invisible to implementers — perpetuating energy gaps despite available solutions.
